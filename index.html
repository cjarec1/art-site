About Me

My name is Kyarou and I'm a 23-year-old college graduate from Colorado currently living in Louisiana! I majored in Computer Science, but art has been a huge passion of mine since I was young. I also love cats and space.

I started streaming on Twitch in July 2017 (although I'm currently on a break), and immediately was drawn to making my stream as aesthetically pleasing as possible. Soon after I realized how much I enjoyed making overlays (as well as other channel art), and wanted to make them for other people as well.

I hope you enjoy what I've created, and if you want to get in touch with me, check out my links under "Find Me."
