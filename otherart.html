Other Art

Prices for art range from $5 to $45, depending on what you want; for example, no shading vs full shading, full body vs waist up, etc. To add an additional character, add half the price (so if it were $14 for one character, add an additional $7). All pictures come with a simple background (a color or image).

Below are examples of my art along with prices! All examples are of females, because I'm bad at drawing males; because of this, prices for drawing a male character are slightly lower. Also: some of the pictured art is a little older, so there's been a lot of improvement. Additionally, if there's a certain format you like but a different style than pictured (for example, lined with cel-shading), let me know!

Things I will not draw: heavy gore, full furries/animals, anything I do not feel comfortable drawing.
